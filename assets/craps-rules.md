# Craps Rules Reference

## Variants

### Standard Craps
- **Come-out naturals:** 7 or 11 — Pass Line wins, Don't Pass loses
- **Come-out craps:** 2, 3 — Pass loses, Don't Pass wins; 12 — Pass loses, Don't Pass pushes
- **Point numbers:** 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 10
- All bet types available

### Crapless Craps
- **Come-out natural:** 7 only — Pass wins
- **Come-out craps:** none — 2, 3, 11, 12 all become point numbers
- **Point numbers:** 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 10, 11, 12
- No Don't Pass / Don't Come
- Come and Buy available (including Buy 2, 3, 11, 12)
- Higher house edge on Pass Line due to extra point numbers

### Easy Craps
- Same come-out rules as Crapless (7 natural; 2,3,11,12 become points)
- No Come, No Don't Pass/Don't Come
- No Buy, No Lay, No Big 6/8
- Adds **Low Field** (wins on 2,3,4 at 2:1) and **High Field** (wins on 10,11,12 at 3:1)
- Simplified for new players learning craps concepts

---

## Come-Out Phase

The shooter rolls before a point is established.

| Roll | Standard | Crapless / Easy |
|---|---|---|
| 7 | Natural (Pass wins) | Natural (Pass wins) |
| 11 | Natural (Pass wins) | Point = 11 |
| 2 | Craps (Pass loses, DP wins) | Point = 2 |
| 3 | Craps (Pass loses, DP wins) | Point = 3 |
| 12 | Craps (Pass loses, DP pushes) | Point = 12 |
| 4,5,6,8,9,10 | Point set | Point set |

## Point Phase

After a point is established:
- **Roll the point** → Pass wins, Don't Pass loses → back to come-out
- **Roll 7 (seven-out)** → Pass loses, Don't Pass wins, working Place/Come bets lose → back to come-out
- **Any other roll** → single-roll bets resolve, Come bets advance, Hardways checked

## Bet Types

### Pass Line
- **Wins:** Natural on come-out; point before 7 in point phase
- **Loses:** Craps on come-out; 7 before point in point phase
- **Payout:** 1:1
- **Locked** after point is set (cannot remove)
- House edge: 1.41%

### Don't Pass
- Opposite of Pass Line (pushes on 12 come-out in Standard)
- Can be **removed** after point set (strategically inadvisable)
- House edge: 1.36%

### Free Odds (Pass/Come)
Zero house edge — best bet in the casino.

| Point | Payout |
|---|---|
| 4 or 10 | 2:1 |
| 5 or 9 | 3:2 |
| 6 or 8 | 6:5 |

### Lay Odds (Don't Pass/Don't Come)

| Point | Payout |
|---|---|
| 4 or 10 | 1:2 |
| 5 or 9 | 2:3 |
| 6 or 8 | 5:6 |

### Odds Multiples

| Setting | Max odds allowed |
|---|---|
| 1x | 1× pass bet |
| 2x | 2× pass bet |
| 3-4-5x | 3× on 4/10, 4× on 5/9, 5× on 6/8 |
| 5x | 5× pass bet |
| 10x | 10× pass bet |

### Come / Don't Come
Work exactly like Pass/Don't Pass but placed after a point is set. Come bets establish their own "come point."

### Place Bets
Win when your number rolls before 7. Can be turned ON/OFF.
Place bets are **OFF by default on come-out** and **ON during point phase**.

| Number | Payout | House Edge |
|---|---|---|
| 4 or 10 | 9:5 | 6.67% |
| 5 or 9 | 7:5 | 4.00% |
| 6 or 8 | 7:6 | 1.52% |
| 2 or 12 (crapless) | 11:2 | ~7.1% |
| 3 or 11 (crapless) | 11:4 | ~6.2% |

### Buy Bets
True odds minus 5% vig. Better than Place on 4/5/9/10.

| Number | Payout |
|---|---|
| 4 or 10 | 2:1 − 5% vig |
| 5 or 9 | 3:2 − 5% vig |
| 6 or 8 | 6:5 − 5% vig |
| 2 or 12 (crapless) | 6:1 − 5% vig |
| 3 or 11 (crapless) | 3:1 − 5% vig |

### Lay Bets
Bet 7 comes before your number. True odds minus 5% vig.

| Number | Payout |
|---|---|
| 4 or 10 | 1:2 − 5% vig |
| 5 or 9 | 2:3 − 5% vig |
| 6 or 8 | 5:6 − 5% vig |

### Big 6 / Big 8
Same as Place 6/8 but pays only 1:1 (house edge 9.09%). Avoid — Place 6/8 at 7:6 is strictly better.

### Field
Single-roll bet. Wins on 2,3,4,9,10,11.
- 3/4/9/10/11 pay 1:1
- 2 pays 2:1
- 12 pays 2:1 (or 3:1 if table offers it)
- Loses on 5,6,7,8
House edge: ~5.6% (2.7% with 3:1 on 12)

### Low Field (Easy Craps only)
Wins on 2, 3, 4 at 2:1. Loses all other rolls.

### High Field (Easy Craps only)
Wins on 10, 11, 12 at 3:1. Loses all other rolls.

### Hardways
Win when the hard combination rolls before the easy way or 7.

| Number | Payout | House Edge |
|---|---|---|
| Hard 4 or Hard 10 | 7:1 | 11.11% |
| Hard 6 or Hard 8 | 9:1 | 9.09% |

### Proposition Bets (single-roll)

| Bet | Wins on | Payout | House Edge |
|---|---|---|---|
| Any 7 | 7 | 4:1 | 16.67% |
| Any Craps | 2, 3, 12 | 7:1 | 11.11% |
| Craps 2 | 2 | 30:1 | 13.89% |
| Craps 3 | 3 | 15:1 | 11.11% |
| Yo 11 | 11 | 15:1 | 11.11% |
| Craps 12 | 12 | 30:1 | 13.89% |
| Hi-Lo | 2 or 12 | 15:1 | 11.11% |
| C&E | 2/3/12 or 11 | 7:1 / 3:1 | 11.11% |

### Horn (4-unit bet)
Split equally across 2, 3, 11, 12. Must be placed in multiples of $4.
- 2 or 12 rolls: win 30:1 on that unit, lose the other 3 units
- 3 or 11 rolls: win 15:1 on that unit, lose the other 3 units
- Any other roll: lose all 4 units

### World (5-unit bet)
Horn + Any 7. Must be placed in multiples of $5.
- Horn numbers: same as Horn on 4 units
- 7: push (win 1 unit at 4:1, lose the 4 Horn units)
- Other rolls: lose all 5 units

### Hop Bets (single-roll)
Bet on an exact dice combination for the next roll.
- **Hard hop** (e.g., 3-3): 30:1
- **Easy hop** (e.g., 2-5): 15:1

## Strategy Summary

| Quality | Bets |
|---|---|
| **Gold — Best value** | Pass/Come/Don't Pass/Don't Come + maximum free odds |
| **White — Acceptable** | Pass/Come without full odds, Place 6/8 |
| **Orange — Poor** | Place 4/5/9/10, Field, Big 6/8 |
| **Red — Avoid** | Hardways, Any 7, Any Craps, Proposition bets, Horn, Hop bets, Low/High Field |

The golden rule: **Pass (or Don't Pass) + maximum free odds** is the lowest house-edge strategy in the casino.
